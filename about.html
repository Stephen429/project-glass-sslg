About Us

Dedicated to serving the student body of Lydia D. Villangca Trade School through transparency, leadership, and accountable action.

The Preamble

"We, the learners of Lydia D. Villangca Trade School, imploring the aid of Divine Providence, believing in the need for a better organized Supreme Secondary Learner Government (SSLG) and in the development of the learners as future leaders of the nation, do hereby promulgate and adopt this Constitution and By-Laws of the Supreme Secondary Learner Government for Secondary Schools that shall advance, implement, and maintain our goals and aspirations, embody the ideals and principles of freedom, equality, justice, and democracy, and promote the welfare of all students and the academic standards of our Alma Mater."

Vision

To be a proactive and transparent student government that empowers every learner to achieve their full potential.

Mission

To uphold the principles of student rights and welfare through active leadership, innovative projects, and accountable governance.

About Project GLASS

Project GLASS (Governance, Leadership, Accountability & Student Services) is an independent digital initiative designed to foster transparency and accessibility within the Supreme Secondary Learner Government (SSLG). This platform serves as your central hub for records, policies, and community initiatives at Lydia D. Villangca Trade School.
